About

A small lab doing work that needs to exist.

Who We Are

Algorithmic Research Group is a for-profit research lab run by @baykenney and a rotating cast of contractors. We're small on purpose.

We build benchmarks, datasets, and infrastructure for evaluating what autonomous AI systems can actually do. Everything we ship is open source. Safety research that can't be independently verified isn't safety research.

We fund the research through consulting work and sponsorships.

How We Work

Small team, open output

One person full-time. Specialists brought in as projects demand. No permanent headcount for its own sake. Just the people the work actually needs.

For-profit, public-interest

We're a for-profit company that publishes everything. The structure keeps us lean and self-directed. The open-source commitment keeps the work honest.

Built to be used

We don't build demos. Our benchmarks, datasets, and tools are designed to be picked up and used by other researchers, auditors, and teams doing real evaluation work.
